Break eggs into a deep bowl, add a little pepper and shake slightly with a fork or whisk. Add the dill, chopped garlic and lightly beat the egg mass again. Wash the zucchini, dry it and grate it on a coarse grater.
Add the grated zucchini to the egg mass and stir. Add flour and mix well again. It is better to salt the zucchini mass just before frying, otherwise the zucchini will release a lot of juice, and the dough will turn out to be liquid.
In a preheated frying pan with vegetable oil, spread the squash mass with a spoon in small slides.
Press down lightly with a spatula or spoon, flattening and shaping as desired. Fry the pancakes over medium heat for 1-2 minutes until golden brown. Turn over, reduce heat, cover and cook for 3-4 minutes, until underside is browned.
